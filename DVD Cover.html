DVD Cover

Energy Matters

Audience:

“Energy Matters” is made to cater for a wide demographic. The age rating for this movie is PG, this thus means that this movie is suitable for children however still has important and meaningful information that would be fitting for adults as well.

Style of Cover:

Regarding the cover for the DVD, I am going to design a cover that shows the need for energy. I’m planning on accomplishing this by having a picture of the energy grid. By doing this, it shows the importance of energy as it demonstrates the complexity and largeness of the energy grid which will only get more complex and larger as technology develops and the world's population increases.

How it was made:

The DVD cover for "Energy Matters" was created using the program Adobe Photoshop. This cover was made by collaborating pictures that I gathered. The spine of this cover has also been blacked out, to make sure that the title "Energy Matters" stands out when in stores. As you can see, this movie features Bill Gates and Elon Musk so it was sensible that a picture of the two renown celebrities were included on this DVD cover as well. The background of those images have also been removed using Photoshop's background eraser tool. The background of this DVD cover is a powergrid that has been edited to possess a soft focus effect which creates a solid foundation to hold all the other components of the cover (increases readability). Finally, the fonts in this DVD cover have also all been sourced from Photoshop.

Does this DVD cover meet your clients needs?

The DVD cover that was made meets the client’s which was accomplished by having one single background image of a power grid which directly relates to energy. Additionally, the DVD cover has a PG rating logo to indicate to the audience that this movie will be appropriate to younger audiences. Finally, I have included the names Elon Musk and Bill Gates as they are known to be related to the worlds demand for energy/ fixing the world’s issues.

How could this DVD cover be improved?

This DVD cover can be improved by making the names Elon Musk and Bill Gates complement the background rather than an eyesore. Additionally, the back of the DVD cover can also be improved to make all the aspects (ie. blurb, images etc) blend into the background hence making it more seamless. Finally, the spine of the DVD cover could also be improved to make it more action-packed and eye catching as it is most likely the first part of the cover that the audience will see.
